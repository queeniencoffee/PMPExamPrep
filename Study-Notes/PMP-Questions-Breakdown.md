# Study Notes – PMP Exam Format

## Overview

The PMP® exam consists of:

- **Total questions:** 180  
  - **175 scored** questions  
  - **5 unscored (pretest)** questions  
- **Total exam time:** **230 minutes** (3 hours 50 minutes) – per PMI (pmi.org)

---

## Domain Breakdown - 
The 180 questions are distributed across three domains:

| Domain              | Weight | Approx. # of Questions |
|---------------------|--------|------------------------|
| **People**          | 42%    | 75 Ques                |
| **Process**         | 50%    | ~90 Ques               |
| **Business Environment** | 8% | ~15 Ques              |
| **Total**           | 100%   | ** 180 Ques **         |

# 155/80 Rules

| Time              | Remaining time | 
|-------------------|----------------|
| 180 Questions          |    230 mins    |
| 120 Questions          |    155 mins    |
| 60 Questions           |    80 mins     |

# ≈ 1 minute 17 seconds per question

---

## Breaks

For the **computer-based exam**, PMI allows:

- **Up to two (2) optional 10-minute breaks**
  - First break: after Question **60** (between Q60 and Q61)
  - Second break: after Question **120** (between Q120 and Q121)

Once you start a break, you **cannot** go back to the questions from the previous section.
